About Us

Who We Are

Welcome to Adopt It. We are a team of passionate pet lovers who are dedicated to finding homes for pets in need. We believe that every pet deserves a loving home and we are committed to helping them find one. Our organisation is non-profit and South Australia based.

Take Rusty. A cute german shepherd who was found wandering the streets of Adelaide. He was hungry, thirsty and scared. He was taken to the local shelter and then to Adopt It. He is now searching for a loving family to bring him in.
